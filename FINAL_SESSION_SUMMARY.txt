### Memory Importance (Phase 2)
```
Formula:
importance = (emotional * 0.4) + (repetition * 0.3) + (recency * 0.3)

Categories:
- CRITICAL:  0.8-1.0  (Store immediately)
- HIGH:      0.6-0.8  (Store soon)
- MEDIUM:    0.4-0.6  (Store if space)
- LOW:       0.0-0.4  (Consider discarding)
```
